🔍 TESTING RATIONALE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Why vol=1.1?
  • Captures institutional low-volume accumulation
  • 30% lower threshold than baseline (₹143K → ₹126K on BANKNIFTY)
  • Real trading shows vol doesn't need to be extreme
  • Confidence gate (80%+) prevents garbage anyway

Why disp=1.4?
  • Slightly higher momentum requirement (1.3 → 1.4)
  • Filters noise and halfway movements
  • Confirms momentum is real, not just small range bar
  • Reduces false breakouts that reverse immediately

Why PERMISSIVE in backtest?
  • Tests actual signal volume passing through system
  • 0% blockage = accurate measurement of all opportunities
  • Confidence-gating still applies
  • Production uses BALANCED for additional safety

Why BALANCED in production?
  • Blocks extreme counter-trend trades (risk reduction)
  • But high-confidence signals (80%+) still pass (opportunity capture)
  • Balanced between safety and performance
